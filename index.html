About Mensura Maat

We are a company specialized in diagnostic engineering, judicial expert services and real estate appraisals. We work with technical rigor, transparency and commitment to truth — no trends, no empty phrases, and no nonsense.

Our Services

  • Technical Reports: Preparation of reports based on detailed surveys, in-person inspections and technical diagnostics, always in compliance with ABNT standards. Includes practical and evidence-based recommendations for decision-making.
  • Judicial Expertise: Work as a court-appointed expert or party-appointed technical assistant in civil and labor lawsuits. Impartial analysis, technically substantiated and written with legal clarity.
  • Property Appraisals: Determination of market value, rental value or residual value of real estate properties, according to NBR 14653 standards. Recognized methodology and updated references.
  • Building Inspections: Complete technical evaluation of buildings regarding safety, maintenance and performance. Ideal for condominiums, businesses, and owners seeking to preserve their assets.
  • Construction Consulting: Technical support in the execution of construction, renovations and remediation of building pathologies. Assistance from planning to delivery, focused on quality, safety and compliance with standards.
